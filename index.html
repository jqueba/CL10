My Journey started here!

April 1st, 2022 You know what? I actually applied for some positions at Accenture before, but for some reason, it didn't work out. But you know what they say - everything happens for a reason! This time around, it worked like a charm and I'm thrilled to be here now!

I was really excited about the opportunity to work here because I believe that this company is a great place for creative and proactive people - just like me! I think there are tons of opportunities to learn and grow, and I can't wait to be a part of it all.


CRUX

Situation: There is a long learning curve to complete a ticket due to the large amount of small tasks involved.
Action: I created a guide to assist the designers in completing their tickets step by step.

  • Created in digital responsive platform
  • Design Leads can keep track of the followed steps
  • Reduce learning curve
  • Standarize processes
  • Defines processes
  • Easy edition to keep it updated

My first assignment was in CRUX. This is an incredible team! I felt so welcome and they've allowed me to create friendships with some amazing people. Not only that, but they've also helped me polish my UI skills.

To give back a little to the team, I started working on a practical initiative that I called the "CRUX Wizard". It's a Microsoft Form that can guide any new hire through the process of completing an assignment for Red Tiger. I wanted to make it easier for others to get up to speed quickly and feel confident in their work.

The idea was to create a simulation of a "chatbot" or companion that could guide new hires during their first assignment days. This companion would work as a helpful guide to assist new hires in getting started and feeling more comfortable with the process.

MCB

Situation: The creation of the visual assets involved several steps in the process.
Action: To automate the process, I created a Photoshop file with smart objects that streamlined the final asset creation with fewer steps.

  • Reduce the proces to complete task
  • Helps to produce automatic and scalable exports.
  • Standardize processes
  • Defines processes

Increasing Productivity was a key focus when I joined this team, and the defining characteristic of the team is its structure. Working alongside some amazing people who have a great sense of what a structured process looks like has been a valuable experience for me.

This team was responsible for a variety of tasks related to Microsoft Events (Envision), including visual design work as part of the process.

I found that some parts of the process were very manual, so to accelerate production and ensure the quality of deliverables, I worked closely with Christhian Arce, Alberto Salazar, and Julio Gonzales to develop Photoshop templates.

By using smart objects and establishing guidelines, we were able to create component assets with a more agile production process.

Visual Identity

Since creating new logos within Accenture and Microsoft was not allowed, it was important to carefully consider the communication of the Academy. We had to work within the existing Accenture and Microsoft guidelines and ensure that any designs did not appear as a new logo.

In order to standardize the visual communication and align with the company guidelines, we recognized the importance of defining Academy Guidelines and how they would be presented to the rest of the studio.

TTo do this, we worked on a micro-brand book that needed to represent the Academy while staying within the Accenture and Microsoft guidelines and policies.

ROSTER

Situation: Design thinking workshops showed that new hires usually feel lost.
Action: With this tool, new hires and managers can have visibility of their teams and capabilities.

  • Created with responsiveness
  • Fast searching by capabilities
  • Increase the networking posibility
  • Helps to meet the teams
  • Organize the posibilities
  • Easier way to find help

Impacting the team: Through the Design Thinking workshops, we have discovered that people in the Studio often feel lost in the current state. Due to virtual communication, many of us have only met our colleagues through Microsoft Teams, which can make it difficult to establish connections and feel a sense of belonging within the team.
