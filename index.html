CRUX

Situation: The amount of small task to complete a ticket has a long learning curve
ACtion: I created a companion to the process

My first assigment was in CRUX. This is an incredible team. I felt very welcome and also allowed me to create friendships with amazing people. Also help me to start polishing my UI skills.

To give back a little to the Team I start working in a practical initiative that I called "CRUX Wizard". This was a Microsoft Form that could guide any new hire trough the process of completing an assigment for Red Tiger.

The idea was to simulate a chatbot or companion for the process. It should works as a guide for the new hire first days.

Visual Identity

Knowing that inside Accenture and Microsoft we could not create new logos. It was important to define the comunication of the Academy. It was needed to work inside the Accenture and Microsoft guidelines but it can not be a new logo

In order to standarize the visual comunication, and having in consideration the company guidelines we knew it was important to define some Academy Guidelines. And how it will be presented to the rest of the studio

To do this we worked in a micro-brandbook. That needed to be undestand as the Academy but staying inside the Accenture guidelines and policies
